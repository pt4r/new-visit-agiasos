The village of Agiasos in Lesvos is my favorite village on the island, even if it's not by the sea. I love it and we visit it every time we're in Lesvos. To stroll through the colorful alleys, for the wonderful old church, for the Greek food on the embers along with the spoon dessert, and for many other points of interest that are worth visiting.

![Agiassos, Lesvos - colorful narrow streets](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-alleys.webp)

Agiasos, Lesvos, is built amphitheatrically at a height of 450m. on the slopes of Mount Olympus. The village is one of the most colorful I have seen in Greece. The walls, doors and windows are painted in bright colors. Of course, the form it has today was acquired in the 20th century.

![Agiassos, Lesvos - colors](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-colors.webp)

In the past, the architecture of the village houses was wooden from the middle up and this part of them protruded towards the narrow streets of the village. However, after a fire that broke out in 1877 destroyed almost the entire village of Agiassos. Thus, the village began to be built slowly again from the beginning.

I don't know which style I would like more, because I love this village just the way it is. The combination of all these colors, with the covered pergolas and the climbing plants that cover the paths and create natural shade are unlike any other place I have visited in our country.

![Agiassos, Lesvos - narrow alley](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-village-street.webp)

## A Village Full of… Art

Now as I say in the title, it is the village of art. Of the art of folk traditions. But, everywhere in every corner of the village you will see shops that sell ceramic art and handmade carved wooden objects. From ashtrays to tables. And all of them are true works of art.

![Agiasos, Lesvos - ceramics](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-ceramics.webp)

![Agiassos, Lesvos - arts](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-arts.webp)

Of course, the colorful ceramics and carved wooden objects are nice and good, but we, as true gluttons that we are, never say no to good local products in Agiasos. So don't miss eating your sweets in one of the cafes. Some of these sweets are Plazenta, Baklavou, the traditional Agiassotiko halva or the delicious sweet spoons. On the other hand, in Agiassos Lesvos you will find to buy some of the most special cheeses, such as touloumotiri or their famous ladotyri in various variations.

![Agiasos, Lesvos - attractions](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-sights.webp)

![Agiassos, Lesvos - traditional art items](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-traditional-crafts.webp)

## The Market Square

The Market Square is located above the Church of the Virgin Mary. An extremely picturesque area, with paved streets and metal pergolas laden with climbing plants that bridge the surrounding alleys. In the middle of it, a deeply shaded plane tree offers its shady coolness to the patrons.

![Agiasos, Lesvos - Market Square](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-plateia.webp)

## The Reading Room

The building complex of the Agiasos Reading Room "Development" is located in the Town Hall square at the lower entrance of the village. Our Cultural Center was founded in 1894 (during the Turkish occupation of Lesvos) and has a library that hosts over 25,000 volumes of books, a 300-seat cinema and a Folklore Museum.

![Agiasos Reading Room](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-anagnostirio-building.webp)

![Agiasos, Lesvos - Reading Room](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-anagnostirio.webp)

## The Cafeteria

The "Cafentaria" building is the hangout we prefer most of the time for coffee and dessert. It is located behind the church of Panagia and is much quieter than the central market. It is owned by the Municipality of Agiasos and has been classified as a historical monument and work of art. It was built in the mid-19th century, a sample of the architecture of the era, with obvious neoclassical elements on the facades.

![Agiasos Lesvos - Cafeteria](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-kafentaria.webp)

## The chapels of Zoodochos Pigi

The chapels of Zoodochos Pigi and Agathon of Ephesus, where according to tradition he built his hermitage. They say that Zoodochos Pigi was the beginning of the current village. Agathon was the one who rescued and brought and hid there the holy relics and the icon of the Virgin Mary. In the chapel we distinguished the beautiful small stone-built bell tower with the clock and its beautifully manicured garden.

![Agiasos, Lesvos - Zoodochos Pigi](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-zoodochos-pigi.webp)

## The Church of the Virgin Mary

Agiasos, Lesvos, is a major attraction for visitors, mainly for the Church of the Virgin Mary, which is located in the center of the Agora. It is a three-aisled basilica, surrounded by high walls, since it used to be a monastery, and dominates approximately the middle of the settlement. Within its precincts there are the Chapel of the Holy Apostles, two Museums (Byzantine and Folk), reception and hospitality areas, warehouses, rental shops, the building complex of the Old Hostel with 65 rooms, the Cultural Center, the two-story "Agia Sion" hotel with 14 rooms and 28 beds and the bell tower.

![Perivolos of Panagia Agiasou](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-perivolos-panagias.webp)

![Temple of Agiasos](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-church.webp)

Every time I look forward to visiting it, to exploring it once again, as its interior is a true work of art. This huge work of ecclesiastical wood carving, the iconostasis with the marble details, the throne and the pulpit. The offerings of the faithful, the Byzantine and post-Byzantine icons, constitute a treasure of unimaginable value. Every time I look forward to visiting the church and filling my mind with new images, as photography inside the church is prohibited.

![Agiassos Lesvos - Church of the Virgin Mary interior](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-church-interior.webp)

Apart from the antiquity of the church, its most precious treasure is the icon of the Virgin Mary holding the Infant Jesus. This icon is kept with absolute reverence in its marble case and dates back over 1100 years. It was brought from Constantinople by the priest Agathon and, according to tradition, is the work of the Evangelist Luke.

The church of Panagia in Agiasos has been rebuilt 3 times after devastating fires. The last time the church was rebuilt was in 1815, which still survives today.

## August 15 in Agiasos

Agiassos, Lesvos celebrates the Assumption of the Virgin Mary in a special way. People start from various parts of the island on foot to visit the church. We had attempted it too and it was a very beautiful experience, except that halfway through my journey my knee got caught and it was impossible for me to continue. Fortunately, a farm truck stopped and put us in its cart and we managed to arrive in Agiasos early in the evening.

![15 August - Route to Agiasos](/assets/images/cms/agiasos-art-colors/agiasos-15-august-map.webp)

In the village on the eve of the day of the Virgin Mary, the unmissable happens. The classic traditional stalls with toys, sweets, corn and souvlaki are set up everywhere. On that day, thousands of visitors arrive in Agiassos to worship the icon of the Virgin Mary or simply to attend the festival. The truth is that we prefer to go early in the morning when there are fewer people to enjoy the beauties of the village in peace.
