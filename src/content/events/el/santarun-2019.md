## <center>If you want to help the kids in need become Santa Claus for a day, <br /> put on your red outfit and your brightest smile and come to Agiasos on Sunday, December 15, 2019 at the most beautiful Christmas solidarity celebration!</center>

The route will take you through the picturesque alleys and end at the Town Hall Square. There is no cost to attend but a piggy bank for "The smile of the Child".

**We invite you to a special Christmas party!**

At the entrance of the Municipal Park at the entrance of the village we welcome children and adults for a series of festive events that combine culture, education and entertainment!

 The Christmas village of Agiasos will not have fancy ornaments but most of the decoration will be made from recyclable and natural materials from the volunteers team of VisitAgiasos.gr with the aim of enhancing the idea of volunteering, recycling and reuse.

Christmas tunes, music, Santa Claus parade, fairy tales, delicious sweets and lots of gold dust from the Elves workshop will compose a unique festive program this Sunday in Agiasos!

We are waiting for you!!!
